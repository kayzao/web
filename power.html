Power Supply


The power supply is a crucial part of any computer, since it provides the only way for a computer to recieve electricity. Power supplies are rates based on how many watts they can provide, and how efficient they are. For example, given a power supply named CX500M 80+ Bronze, its efficiency is at minimum above 80%, so if it is providing 500 watts, it is drawing a maximum of 625 watts from the wall. Bronze is an additional categorization, with Bronze being the least efficient 80+ PSU, followed by silver, gold, platinum and finally, titanium. The more efficient the power supply, the less electricity is being wasted.
